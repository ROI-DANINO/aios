## Who They Are (Hypothesis)

**SMB owners and solo operators** — typically 1–5 person businesses — who:
- Run primarily on their own judgment and context (no ops team)
- Are drowning in unstructured tasks: emails, notes, decisions, research, recurring ops
- Have tried productivity tools (Notion, ClickUp, Zapier) and found them either too rigid or too empty
- Are comfortable with technology but not necessarily developers
- Spend most of their day reacting instead of building

## Pain
They have no system. Everything lives in their head or scattered across tools. When they're "in it," nothing gets documented. When they step back to organize, they lose momentum. They need an AI that knows them and works in the background — not another tool they have to feed.

## Signal (When They're Ready)
- "I've tried Notion/Linear/X and I'm still behind"
- "I know what I need to do, I just can't get to it"
- "I want an AI that actually knows my business"
- Actively using Claude or ChatGPT but copy-pasting context every time

## What They're NOT
- Enterprise / team of 10+
- Technical builders who want to DIY (they'll build their own)
- People looking for a SaaS subscription to a pre-built tool

## Why AIOS Fits
Configured to know their business: voice, goals, ICP, ops. Speaks as them. Handles ops in background. Owner stays in creative flow.
